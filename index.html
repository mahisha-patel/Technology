Technology has always played a drastic role in our lives, but it played a mandatory role in lockdown.

Some areas where technology played major role in this lockdown:

Schools/Colleges

Online teaching was so in trend, adopted video lectures, online submissions, online exams/tests, webinars and so on.

Offices/Workplaces

Adoption of online platforms to work from home and even for the meetings, conferences and seminars.

Hospitals

As it was not safe to go to hospital, patients were consulting their doctors through video calls and phone calls.

Courts

Even the court was started on online platform, the hearings and the judgements were given via video conferencing.

Entertainment

All the actors, musicians, comedians, dancers, etc. entertained their fans via live videos on television and social media.

Other areas

More than that teaching of various other activities like yoga, dancing, singing, etc. started via online platforms.



Technology in need :
AAROGYA SETU App

Aarogya setu app is the best use of technology in this period. It provides Contact tracing, Syndromic mapping and Self-assessment digital service.It tells how many COVID-19 positive cases are likely in a radius of 500 m, 1 km, 2 km, 5 km and 10 km from the user.

Aarogya Setu has four sections:
User Status (tells the risk of getting COVID-19 for the user)
Self Assess (helps the users identify COVID-19 symptoms and their risk profile)
COVID-19 Updates (gives updates on local and national COVID-19 cases)
E-pass integration (If applied for E-pass, it will be available)

Entertainment


Imagine this lockdown in the 90's and not having any entertainment things at home?? Wouldn't it be difficult to survive these days??

The movies, webseries and all really helped people to stay at home in this difficult pandemic situation and spend their time watching these with their family and loved ones. Having to stay at home all day, it's so relaxing that one can enjoy this things and spend their time happily!

This is just possible because of technology that we can enjoy this things sitting merely at home.


Drones/CCTV Surveillance


As it was difficult to handle this situation for police, they started seeking help from the drones as well as cctv's to keep an eye on the area and to be sure that no one steps out of their homes and stay safe from the COVID-19 spreaders. This is just a blessing as it is not possible for the police teams to stay at every places , especially when this disease spreads from contact and have to keep social distancing.

Apart from this, drones were also used in delivering food by well known food delivering organizations.

Indeed, Technology is a blessing:


Whether it is about social cause or entertainment, technology has always became a boon for entire world.

The airplanes through which people were able to reach their home safely from different parts of the world, the equipments through which testing the patients of virus are possible, the machines which produced numerous essential materials like face masks and hand gloves, are all a part of blooming technology.

Believe it or not, it wouldn't have been possible to stand and fight against this huge pandemic without having all these facilities!!
